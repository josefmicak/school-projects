What is Dota 2?

Dota 2 is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) video game. Hundreds of thousands of players all over the world play the game every day.

What is the purpose of the game?

Players play against each other in 5-man teams, 2 teams are competing at the time. The purpose is to destroy enemy team's ancient.

Who is behind the game's development?

The game is being developed by a known American video game developer company, Valve Corporation.
